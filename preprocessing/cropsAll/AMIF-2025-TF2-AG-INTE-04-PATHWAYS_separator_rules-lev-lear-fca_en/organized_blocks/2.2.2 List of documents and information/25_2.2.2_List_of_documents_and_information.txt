2.2.2 List of documents and information

The participants will be asked to submit the following documents and information:

1. Signed legal entity identification form’:

— Natural Person Form: in English — Private Legal Entity Form: in English — Public Legal Entity Form: in English.

2. Official VAT document or — if the entity is not registered for VAT — the proof of VAT exemption, not older than one (1) year.
