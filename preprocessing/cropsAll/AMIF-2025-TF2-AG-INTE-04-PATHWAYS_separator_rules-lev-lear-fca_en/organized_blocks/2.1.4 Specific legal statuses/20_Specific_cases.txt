Specific cases

« Entities set up as distinct bodies by an international organisation with a special mission but which do not have explicit legal personality — Can be validated as with legal personality, if they meet any of the following criteria:

— they have capacity to act at international level independently from the organisation that set them up (i.e. capacity to undertake legal obligations).

If such entities do not meet one of the above criteria, they can exceptionally be validated as without legal personality (see section 2.1.3 above).
