5.3 Duration of validity and renewal

The ownership control assessment is in principle valid for 18 months. If during this period the participant applies for another grant or tender and confirms that the ownership control status has not changed, the supporting documents will not be requested again.

4) In this case, the participants will still have to submit the ownership control declaration with the application, but there will be no check by the Central Validation Service, unless there are justified grounds to doubt the declaration.
