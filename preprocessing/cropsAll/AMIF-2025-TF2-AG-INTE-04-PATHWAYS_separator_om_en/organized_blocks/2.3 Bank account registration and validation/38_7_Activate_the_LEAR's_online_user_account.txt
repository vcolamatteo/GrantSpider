7 Activate the LEAR's online user account

After validation, the LEAR account (with the special access rights) can be be activated by:

 • following the instructions the LEAR receives by e-mail

 • using the account activation PIN code which will be sent to the LEAR by post, or by SMS if the LEAR's mobile phone number was provided

Should you encounter any problems with the LEAR appointment, please contact the Central Validation Service via the Portal Messaging Facility (My Organisations > Actions> Modify Organisation).
