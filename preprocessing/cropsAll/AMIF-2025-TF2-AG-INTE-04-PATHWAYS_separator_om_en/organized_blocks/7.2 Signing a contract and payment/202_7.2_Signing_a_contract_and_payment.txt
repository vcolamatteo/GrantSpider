7.2 Signing a contract and payment

Contracts

If you are selected as an expert, you will be offered a contract to be signed and managed through the Funding & Tenders Portal. This contract (including its annexes) defines all the rights, obligations, terms and conditions applicable to you. It is not an employment contract or agreement.

Your contract will be based on the Mode! Contract for Experts. Some of the terms and conditions in your contract will vary, depending on the type of expert activity that you are asked to carry out. The contract will indicate

 • whether or not your work will involve travel,

 • the maximum number of days of work that you will be paid for and  • the planned calendar for your work.
